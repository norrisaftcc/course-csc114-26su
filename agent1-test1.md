# Response
CSC 114 – Artificial Intelligence I
Great question! Here's a quick overview of the course:

📚 What is it?
CSC 114 – Artificial Intelligence I is a 3-credit introductory AI course offered at Fayetteville Technical Community College (FTCC). It covers foundational concepts in artificial intelligence using hands-on labs and lectures.

Format: In-person (Traditional)
Schedule: Monday & Wednesday, 10:00–11:50 AM (Lecture + Lab)
Location: Advanced Technology Center (ATC), Room 115
Term: May 26 – July 20, 2026
Prerequisite: CSC-113


💻 Would it be useful for a CS student?
Absolutely! Here's why:

🤖 AI is everywhere — machine learning, computer vision, and NLP are core skills in today's tech industry
🛠️ Hands-on labs give you practical experience, not just theory
📖 The textbook (Deep Learning with Python, 3rd Ed. by François Chollet & Matthew Watson) is highly regarded in the AI/ML field
📋 Topics include AI Frameworks, Classification & Regression, Machine Learning Workflows, Computer Vision, and NLP/LLMs — all highly relevant to modern CS careers


⚠️ One thing to note:
You'll need CSC-113 as a prerequisite, so make sure you've completed that first!
